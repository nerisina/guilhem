Place the Sublime Text snippets inside your app folder.

the path must be something like this:

..\user\AppData\Roaming\Sublime Text 2\Packages

Or open your Sublime Text click - Preferences - Browser Packages and place the snippets
inside their specific folders:

folder- HTML for the "responsiveBoilerplate-HTML5-Template"
folder- CSS for the "responsiveBoilerplate-CSS-Grid"

Now restart the editor!
